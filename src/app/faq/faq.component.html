Are there any tax benefits on education loans for studying abroad?

Yes, there are tax benefits for education loans.
Under Section 80E of the Income Tax Act, you can claim a deduction on the interest paid on an education loan for up to 8 years. There is no upper limit on the amount of interest you can claim.
Tax Collected at Source (TCS) = 0, during the time of remittance if you utilize funds from education loans, as per Budget 2025.
